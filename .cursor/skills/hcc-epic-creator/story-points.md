# Story Point Scale

Story points estimate effort, complexity, and risk — regardless of who is doing the work.

| Points | Definition | Effort & Complexity | Examples |
|--------|-----------|---------------------|----------|
| 1 | Extremely straightforward task | Very low effort, no risk | Update spacing, fix a typo, export assets |
| 2 | Straightforward work, little risk, little to no investigation needed | Low effort, clear solution, low risk | Tweak wireframes, create a simple user flow |
| 3 | Can be time consuming but fairly straightforward. Some minor risks. Some collaboration or iteration might be needed | Moderate effort, low risk | Draft a new screen, update a pattern, prepare for a review |
| 5 | Probably requires investigation, discussions, and collaboration. Time consuming or complex. Risks involved | High effort or moderate complexity | Create a new user experience flow. Conduct user interviews and synthesize findings |
| 8 | Big task requiring investigation, collaboration + discussion. Solution can be quite challenging. Risks expected. Consider breaking down into smaller tickets | Large, complex, or unclear. High risk | Full pattern definition. Multi-feature research |
| 13 | Too big — split it up. Should not be used; break into smaller tasks | Not sprint-sized | Break down before pointing |

## PTO / Holiday Adjustments

| Missed Days | Story Point Adjustment |
|-------------|----------------------|
| Half day | -2 story points |
| 1 day | -3 story points |
| 2-3 days | -5 story points |
| 4-6 days | -8 story points |
| Full sprint (3 weeks) | -20 story points |
