# UXD Activity Types by Phase

Activity types track UX phases in Jira to assess and monitor UX maturity across product areas.

## Phases

### Enable
Work internal to the UXD team intended to enable UXDers to be happier or more effective.

**Outputs:** Documentation for an internal process, a change in how we do something.

### Consult
Emergent work prompted by a team external to UXD. Might be a quick chat or could turn into a larger, separate work item.

**Outputs:** A comment on an issue or PR review, a meeting, another issue describing further work, a wireframe or update for a Bug.

### Orient (Project Work)
Identify opportunities and plan. Understand the user and their goals. Identify the problem to pursue and desired outcomes. Efforts are in very early stages.

**Outputs:** Quarterly roadmaps, early planning workshop artifacts, user problems/stories, documented set of problems, a problem statement with prioritized business outcomes.

### Explore (Project Work)
Generate ideas — what are potential solutions for delivering the outcome?

**Outputs:** Ideation and initial design work, journey maps/service blueprints, user research, concept storyboards, prioritized list of design stories.

### Make (Project Work)
Finalize the design. Prototype, test, and iterate.

**Outputs:** Testable prototypes, multiple solution ideas, detailed designs and specs ready to implement, evaluative research.

### Monitor (Project Work)
Measure — how are our solutions performing over time?

**Outputs:** Analytics and metrics, usability benchmark results, desk research or analysis of user behavior and feature adoption post-implementation.

## Choosing a Phase

| Signal | Phase |
|--------|-------|
| "We need to figure out what the problem is" | Orient |
| "We're exploring ideas and doing research" | Explore |
| "We're designing and prototyping solutions" | Make |
| "We're measuring how it's working" | Monitor |
| "This is internal team process work" | Enable |
| "Another team asked us to review/help" | Consult |
