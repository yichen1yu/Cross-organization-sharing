3. High dismiss rate (~83%): The majority of users close the guide without interacting. This is typical for unsolicited in-app prompts but worth monitoring over time.

Recommendations

Investigate the high dismiss/rage-close rates — consider adjusting when and where the guide appears (e.g., only on the Subscriptions page rather than broadly across the console).
Monitor the conversion trend — the early days (Mar 27–29) had higher conversion rates (~19–21%) with lower volume, suggesting more targeted audiences convert better. The broader rollout reduced conversion to ~15–17%.
Consider making the snooze option more prominent — this could help recapture dismissed users on a future visit rather than losing them permanently.
